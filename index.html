About Me

Game Developer

My name is Celtia Martín García and I'm from Arzúa in A Coruña (Galicia, Spain), but I'm living in Móstoles (Madrid, Spain) right now. I have a double degree in Design and Development of Videogames and Computer Engineering at the Rey Juan Carlos University. I have a year and a half of experience in the industry: I've worked for Sepia Games in the development of Dread Swipes, and for Xaloc Studios (Palma de Mallorca), developing a couple of projects. Also, I'm currently working at Save Games Studio (Sevilla), since November 2022. I love being part of the game development world and I am always eager to learn more about it.
